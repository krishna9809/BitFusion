01

Medium of Exchange

Crypto tokens can serve as a digital currency for transactions within a specific blockchain network or ecosystem. Users can exchange tokens as a means of payment for goods and services, similar to traditional currencies.

02

Access to Decentralized Applications

Tokens are often used to access and interact with decentralized applications (DApps) built on blockchain platforms. Users may need to hold or spend tokens to use specific features or services within these DApps.

03

Staking and Rewards

Tokens may offer staking capabilities, enabling users to lock up their tokens as collateral to secure the network or participate in network consensus mechanisms. In return, stakers may receive rewards in the form of additional tokens.

04

Utility Within Ecosystems

Tokens often have specific utility within their respective ecosystems. For example, they may be required to pay transaction fees, access premium features, or participate in ecosystem-specific services like decentralized finance (DeFi) lending and borrowing.
